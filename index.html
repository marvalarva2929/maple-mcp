How it works

Three steps, zero friction

1

Connect Maple

One command adds Maple as an MCP server to Claude Code. No account needed, no signup.

2

Code normally

When you ask your agent for a tool recommendation, it checks Maple for exclusive discounts relevant to your stack.

3

Click the link, done

Your agent shares a link. Click it — the company recognises you came through Maple and applies the deal automatically on their site.
